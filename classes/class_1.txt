class Dish
Attributes:
1. cuisine (array)
2. spice_level (array)
3. price_in_dollars (float)
4. contain_nuts? (boolean)
Method:
1. change_cusine (changes the cuisine of the dish ordered)
2. reduce_spice (reduces the spice content of the dish ordered)
3. total_price (add tip to after-tax price to arrive at the final price)
4. remove_nuts (remove nuts from dish if boolean returned is true for attribute contain_nuts?)
